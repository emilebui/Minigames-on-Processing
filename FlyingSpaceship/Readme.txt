How to play:

Hold A,D to move left and right

Hold W,S to move up and down

Every second you survive, you get a point
Take purple rune to get 10 points

If you get hit by other objects, you die

Press SPACEBAR to change the view

Press R then wait for the game to reload to restart

Textures taken from https://www.pexels.com/
